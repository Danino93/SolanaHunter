## 📊 Position Sizing

### The Golden Rule:
**אף פעם לא יותר מ-5% מהתיק בסחר אחד**

### מדרג Position Size לפי Score:
- Score 95-100: 5% of portfolio
- Score 90-94:  4% of portfolio
- Score 85-89:  3% of portfolio
- Score 80-84:  2% of portfolio
- Score < 80:   SKIP

### Maximum Exposure:
- Max open positions: 5
- Max total exposure: 25%

## 🎬 Entry Strategy - The 30-40-30 DCA

**Never buy all at once!**

Stage 1: 30% → Enter NOW  
Wait 2 minutes  
Stage 2: 40% → If price stable/up  
Wait 2 minutes  
Stage 3: 30% → If volume spiking

## 🚪 Exit Strategy - The 30-30-40 Rule
